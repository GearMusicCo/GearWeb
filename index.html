About Me

Geoffrey is a seasoned tenor with decades of experience across various musical genres including opera, musical theatre, jazz, and contemporary music. Native to Columbus, Ohio, Geoffrey's love for music began in the early 2010's while performing tenor for Hillard Highschools choir. {{correct?}} He then carried on his passion to Otterbein University in Westerville, Ohio, where he completed his Bachelor's degree in Music {{correct?}} where he learned to play piano and began teaching his own students. {{correct?}} Currently he is attending The Ohio State University in Columbus Ohio where he is pursuing his Master's in music.

As a voice teacher, Geoffrey offers a wealth of knowledge and experience in a wide array of vocal styles. He is well-versed in modern musical theatre and belt techniques, as well as classical and jazz methods. His teaching philosophy centers on providing individually curated lessons, designed to help each student unlock their full potential and bring their unique voice to life. Geoffrey’s ability to connect with students of all levels, combined with his deep understanding of vocal technique, makes him an ideal mentor for anyone looking to elevate their singing. Whether you're aiming to refine your classical technique, master the nuances of jazz, or develop a powerful belt for musical theatre, Geoffrey’s personalized approach ensures that your lessons will be tailored to your specific goals and aspirations. In addition to his teaching, Geoffrey is an exceptional choice for live performances at weddings, corporate events, and other special occasions. His rich, expressive voice and dynamic stage presence guarantee an unforgettable experience for your guests, bringing any event to life with the perfect musical touch. Book Geoffrey for your next event or take your singing to the next level with lessons that inspire and empower.
